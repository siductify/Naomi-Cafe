Cafe Story

It started in a small garage in the heart of Mumbai with a single vintage espresso machine and a dream to bring authentic, slow-roasted beans to the neighborhood. Founded by Rohan and Meera, The Velvet Bean wasn't just about coffee; it was about the community.

Today, we stand as a sanctuary for artists, thinkers, and lovers of fine taste. Every brick in our wall tells a story of late nights, perfect grinds, and the countless smiles shared over a warm cup of magic.
