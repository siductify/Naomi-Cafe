Visual Journey

How it began

2018: The Seed

Inspired by the quiet coffee culture of Kyoto, Naomi started roasting small batches in a backyard in Brooklyn, seeking the perfect balance of acidity and soul.

2020: The Brick and Mortar

Amidst a changing world, we opened our first doors. We wanted to create a sanctuary—a place where time slows down just enough for you to taste the notes of jasmine and chocolate.

2023: Community Growth

Naomi Cafe evolved into more than a coffee shop. It became a hub for local artists, a workspace for dreamers, and a home for anyone who appreciates the craft of a manual brew.

Today: The Journey Continues

Every cup we serve is a chapter of our story. We continue to source ethically, roast meticulously, and serve with heart.
